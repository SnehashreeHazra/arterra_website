MUMBAI BANDRA HOUSE DESIGN

What is the significance of lotus in interior design? The stem is our connection to our roots, while the blossomed flower represents the spiritual enlightenment that we seek reflecting prosperity, beauty & purity. We, at Arterra used the concept of a blossoming lotus running throughout the house floor to represent the fluidity of life and how a home being an extended representation of ourselves, reminds us to follow the organic flow of nature. A home tells the story of who you are – and that was exactly our design intent for this special project.

Not only on the floors, the design also stretches out on the walls and windows to seamlessly blend the tranquility of nature in the everyday hustle & bustle. We chose a single color of gold as it not only signifies growing prosperity, but also adds the desire break from white of the marble. The intricate art of Marble Inlay, the exquisite craftsmanship of carefully cutting and engraving marble shapes by hand also evident in all historic monuments in India, was used to reflect the richness and delicacy of the space. These days, this style of design is rarely seen as it is a closely protected traditional art and only a few experts are skilled enough to do justice to it.

At Arterra, we decided to incorporate this age-old method of designing in a new project, where the design intent was to seamlessly blend in the timeless art of marble inlay with a modern minimalistic theme for the rest of the house.
