Glitch – Transformation

Glitch-Transformation is an unconventional perspective of design, where glitches in a computer software can be tweaked to harnesses mutations to profitably render evolution.

The GIF demonstrates how glitch, a computer malfunction, can be used as a design tool in order to create objects to generate highly aesthetical transforming objects creating building blocks for a futuristic city inhabited by parasites.

Our goal through this research project was to explore innovative, potential architectural expressions, material intelligence, formal logic efficiencies and precision assemblies as an ultimate condition of design.

The animation demonstrates the processes that allow for control over intelligent geometries, calibration of parts, and behavioral taxonomies, normalizing an innovative field of predictability. The end process resulting in a technology driven futuristic city.
