Concept 1

White is the color of peace, tranquility, simplicity and innocence. In Interior Design and Space Planning, White is predominantly used to denote space, cleanliness and hygiene. Combined with marble, it adds to the smooth transition and movement by reflecting all light from the walls and ceiling making the space appear larger. At Arterra, we have designed one such modern condominium playing with Earthy Tunes and sticking to the traditional White Monochromatic pattern allowing free flow of natural light.

This penthouse apartment in the heart of Mumbai was designed to give a sense of expanse to the suite with a clean minimalistic look blending into the sea view. The use of yellow and gold adds to the richness of the space while white acts as a balancer, adding to the simplicity of the design. Yellow is the color of happiness and warmth, complementing the starkness of white. The touch of wood adds warmth to the space allowing fresh flow of light and air!
