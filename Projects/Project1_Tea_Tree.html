Tea Tree Café -2019

Arterra conceptualized and designed a modern minimalistic café at the Annual Flower Show celebration at the AHSI. The café named The Tree Café, boasted a natural and eco-friendly structure which imbued the ethos that Arrtera stands for.

Arterra presented the Tea Tree Café at the Annual Flower Show celebration of the 200 year-old Agri-Horticultural Society of India. This café served visitors an indulgence in healthy, organic, farm-to-table treats, delectable street food and various cooking demonstrations on healthy organic plant based foods for modern day living.

Tea Tree Café -2020

The year 2020 marked another milestone for the café as it partnered with Starbucks and celebrated the Bicentenary Flower Show at AHSI.

While the coffee giant was busy serving piping hot latte, the café continued its menu of signature healthy snacks keeping the visitors busy at the venue while enjoying the phenomenal display of flowers around.
