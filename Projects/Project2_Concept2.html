Concept 2

Since the Stone Age, wood has been the go-to construction material for builders and designers. It’s also more sustainable. Wood being a living organism, it contains a spiritual energy that affects us in a positive way and helps create a relaxing environment. Wood’s Zen-like presence can be found everywhere in interior design. Its natural organic properties make it one of the most versatile elements evident in all good design, and a simple way to bring nature to every room of your home.

In this second set of design ideation for the sea facing apartment, we tried playing with material instead of color to bring in the natural element. Apart from the traditional use of wood in furniture, we showcased the multi functionality of the material by using it as a wall cladding. The teal upholstery adds to the drama of the space, balancing the simplicity of the design intent. This unique combination lives up to the story of a bespoke luxury apartment in the heart of Mumbai.
