Commercial Kitchen

Kitchen is that place where Maestros bring alive the food for the soul! So naturally, when it comes to designing a kitchen space it requires an equal amount of thought and planning. In the age of social distancing, Cloud Kitchen is going to be the new booming industry. Our founder at Arterra being a chef herself, believes that a kitchen should not only be designed to meet the end needs but also create a synergy between the design of the space and the kind of food being prepared.

The images here show how we have seamlessly blended a raw, rustic industrial style with a clean minimalistic design to allow the flow of clean positive energy into the powerhouse for our body.
